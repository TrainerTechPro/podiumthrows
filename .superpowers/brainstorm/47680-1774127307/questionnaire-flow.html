Questionnaire Flow Structure

How should the athlete move through the self-programming questionnaire?

A

Single Long Form (Sectioned)

One continuous questionnaire broken into labeled sections. Athlete scrolls through or navigates sections: Event Info → Experience → Training Availability → Equipment → Goals → Preferences. Progress bar at top. Can jump between sections.

B

Step-by-Step Wizard

One question (or small group) per screen. Animated transitions between steps. Feels focused and guided — like an onboarding flow. Back/forward navigation. Can save progress and resume later.

Conversational / Adaptive

Questions appear one at a time, and later questions adapt based on earlier answers. E.g., if athlete picks "Throws + Lifting", lifting-specific questions appear. If they have typing data, skip those questions. Feels personalized but takes longer to build.
