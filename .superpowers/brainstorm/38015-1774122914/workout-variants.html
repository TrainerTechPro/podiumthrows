How Beta Testing Works
Each athlete gets randomly assigned a variant (stored as a cookie/preference). After 3 sessions with that variant, the feedback widget appears. Athletes rate 1-5 stars and optionally comment. Coach dashboard shows aggregated results. After the test period, you pick the winner and ship it to everyone.
