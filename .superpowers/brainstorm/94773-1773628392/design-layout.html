Key Layout Decisions

Full-bleed images
Both images span the entire hero section edge-to-edge. No container constraints. The mask reveal works anywhere the cursor goes — maximum immersion.
Left-anchored text
Text stays in the left ~40% with a strong dark gradient behind it. Right side is where the imagery shines and the mask has room to play.
Double gradient overlay
Left-to-right gradient (85% → transparent) for text. Bottom-to-top gradient for the section transition. Text always readable even when mask passes near it.
Hero height: 100svh
Full viewport height. The hero IS the first impression. Header overlays the top. Scroll indicator at the bottom hints there's more below.
